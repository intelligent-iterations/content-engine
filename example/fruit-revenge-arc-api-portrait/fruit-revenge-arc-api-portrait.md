# Bullied Orange Serum Revenge Cafeteria Drama

Template: Anthropomorphic Fruit Revenge Drama

## Format Summary
Ozzie Orange is a bumpy-rind cafeteria loser who gets publicly humiliated by Lola Lemon and her polished fruit clique in front of the whole school. He buys a sketchy mutation serum from Silas Banana, poisons the clique's lunch, and watches them turn hideous in the middle of the cafeteria. The final flip is social and visual: Scarlet Cherry, the gorgeous girl who saw everything, walks past the ruined bullies and chooses Ozzie instead. The silhouettes, props, and school setting make every beat instantly readable on mobile.

## Fruit Cast Sheet
Ozzie Orange is the wronged lead: bright orange rind, visible bumps and pores, worried brown eyes, faded navy varsity jacket, cafeteria polo, dark jeans, white sneakers, green lunch-card lanyard. He should look awkward, soft, and embarrassed until the revenge lands. Lola Lemon is the bully queen: glossy perfect yellow peel, icy blue eyes, cat-eye lashes, cropped white cheer jacket, metallic gold skirt, glossy white boots, bracelets, and a glitter phone case chain. She is gorgeous and cruel. Silas Banana is the shady dealer: bruised banana edges, narrow green eyes, black faux-leather jacket, dark hoodie, fingerless gloves, cargo pants, beat-up sneakers, chain wallet, and neon serum vials. Scarlet Cherry is the stunning reward/status-flip: glossy crimson finish, elegant twin stem curl, amber-brown eyes, black off-shoulder top, crimson satin mini skirt, tights, tall black boots, gold earrings, and a silver heart necklace.

## Justice Mechanic
The revenge device is a glowing neon-green serum sold by Silas Banana behind the cafeteria. Ozzie secretly loads one premium lunch tray with the serum, then makes a clear physical tray switch so Lola grabs the treated tray without realizing it. When the clique eats, the serum causes an explosive grotesque mutation: split peels, asymmetrical swelling, glam collapse, leaking pulp, neon foam, and cafeteria-food chaos erupt at the table in front of everyone. The payoff is shocking, absurd, and easy to read in one instant.

## Location Lock
The main world is a bright high-school cafeteria with skylight panels, fluorescent strips, chrome lunch tables, polished tile floors, colorful juice posters, lunch trays, and reflective sneeze guards. The secondary location is the dirty service alley behind the cafeteria with dumpsters, wet concrete, service lights, and steam from kitchen vents. Keep the whole story in the same school universe, mixing harsh cafeteria brightness with grimy alley sodium light so the revenge feels local and immediate rather than cinematic in a random new place.

## Asset Plan
Lock four recurring cast assets first: Ozzie Orange, Lola Lemon, Silas Banana, and Scarlet Cherry each need one approved hero portrait and one derived turnaround sheet. Then build five scene start frames from those references: the cafeteria bullying, the alley serum deal, the lunch-tray poisoning, the mutation shock payoff, and the final hand-taking status flip. Clips 1, 3, 4, and 5 need multiple attached character references because the social hierarchy matters visually. Clip 2 can stay focused on Ozzie and Silas. Clip 4 is the shock hero beat, and clip 5 must preserve the exact mutation aftermath in the background while Cherry chooses Ozzie in front of everyone.

## Story Beats
1. Ozzie gets humiliated for his bumpy peel in the cafeteria.
2. He buys a glowing serum from shady Silas Banana behind the school.
3. He swaps Lola's clean tray for the serum-loaded tray while Cherry notices.
